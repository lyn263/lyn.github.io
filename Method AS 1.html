Cooking Method

1

Clean the chicken and put in a pot to boil until tender.
Take out the chicken. Chicken broth should not be discarded.

2

While waiting for the chicken to cook,
you can wash the rice clean. Put the chicken broth
into the rice cooker.

3

Cook the rice until fluffy. When the porridge is
almost cooked, add ginger,
chicken stock, a little salt, black pepper.

4

Drizzle a little sesame oil to taste.Porridge can
be served with the chicken.
